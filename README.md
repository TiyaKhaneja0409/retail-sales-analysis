# Retail Sales Analysis

An end-to-end sales data analysis project using Python and Excel/Google Sheets, exploring regional performance, category trends, and sales growth over time.

## Overview
Analyzed a retail sales dataset (~9,800 orders) to uncover patterns in regional performance, product category sales, and monthly trends using both Python and spreadsheet-based analysis.

## Key Insights
- **Regional Performance:** West and East regions lead in total sales, together contributing significantly more than Central and South — highlighting a regional performance gap worth investigating.
- **Category Performance:** Technology is the top-selling category, closely followed by Furniture and Office Supplies, showing a fairly balanced product mix.
- **Sales Growth Trend:** Monthly sales show clear month-to-month volatility, but a strong overall upward trend over the ~4 year period, with peak months growing significantly higher in later years compared to early data.

## Tools & Methods
- **Python (Pandas, Matplotlib):** Data cleaning, groupby aggregations, and trend visualization in Jupyter Notebook
- **Excel / Google Sheets:** Pivot tables for Region, Category, and Monthly Sales Trend, plus a line chart for trend visualization
